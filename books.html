I am Sorry (2018)

Fiction Thriller

The city of sin was in decline on a stormy and dark evening as news channels became politicized and police prepared their weapons. A live video was being streamed on all major social media platforms, and the entire country was watching as a potential massacre unfolded. The video showed only a single person sitting in a dark room, whispering, "I am sorry."
